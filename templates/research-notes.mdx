---

Use this template when diving into a topic that needs more than a quick search. It helps you frame your research question upfront, record sources as you go, and synthesize findings into a clear takeaway.

```markdown research-notes-template.md
# Research: [Topic]

**Date started:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Researcher:** Your Name  
**Status:** In Progress / Complete  

## Research Question

What specific question are you trying to answer?

## Background

Brief context on why this research is needed.

## Sources

| Source | Type | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| [Title](URL) | Article / Doc / Video | One-line summary |

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: [Headline]

- Supporting detail
- Supporting detail

### Finding 2: [Headline]

- Supporting detail

## Gaps & Open Questions

- What still needs investigation?

## Summary & Recommendation

One paragraph: what did you learn, and what should happen next?
```
