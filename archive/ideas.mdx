This is your ideas backlog — a low-pressure space to capture thoughts before they're ready to become real projects. Dump ideas here freely, then review quarterly to promote the best ones to active Projects.

## How to Use This Page

1. Add ideas freely — don't filter yourself. An imperfect idea is better than a forgotten one.
2. Include enough context that you'll understand it when you return in 3 months.
3. Review quarterly: promote, defer, or delete each item.
