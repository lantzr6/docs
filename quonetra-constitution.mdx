# Quonetra Constitution

### Version 1.0

> **Purpose**
>
> This Constitution defines the enduring philosophy, principles, and boundaries of the Quonetra project.
>
> It is the authoritative source of truth for the project.
>
> Every architectural decision, implementation, experiment, feature, and supporting document must align with this Constitution.
>
> If any supporting document contradicts this Constitution, the Constitution takes precedence unless it is intentionally amended.

---

# 1. Mission & Vision

## Mission

Quonetra exists to transform evidence into trustworthy probabilistic intelligence.

Its purpose is to continuously estimate the true probability of future events, explain the reasoning behind those estimates, and identify statistically favorable opportunities for decision makers.

Quonetra exists to improve understanding—not to automate decisions.

## Vision

Build an AI platform that continually becomes better at understanding uncertain events.

Rather than simply making predictions, Quonetra should continuously:

- Observe
- Learn
- Experiment
- Measure
- Accumulate knowledge
- Improve itself

Its greatest asset should not be a model, a prompt, or an algorithm.

Its greatest asset should be accumulated knowledge.

---

# 2. Foundational Principles

Everything is measurable.

Everything is an experiment.

Evidence over intuition.

Knowledge compounds.

Configuration over code.

Version everything.

Explain every recommendation.

Improve continuously.

No production change without evidence.

Reproducibility is mandatory.

---

# 3. Project Philosophy

Quonetra is fundamentally a scientific reasoning platform.

It observes the world.

It forms hypotheses.

It evaluates evidence.

It estimates probabilities.

It measures outcomes.

It learns from experience.

Every prediction is provisional.

Every assumption may be challenged.

Every improvement must be supported by measurable evidence.

---

# 4. What Quonetra Is

Quonetra is a Prediction Intelligence Platform.

Its responsibilities include:

- Collecting evidence
- Normalizing information
- Estimating probabilities
- Measuring confidence
- Synthesizing information
- Explaining reasoning
- Identifying statistically favorable opportunities
- Learning from outcomes
- Running experiments
- Accumulating reusable knowledge

Quonetra produces intelligence.

It does not execute decisions.

---

# 5. What Quonettra Is Not

Quonetra is not an automated trading bot.

Quonetra is not portfolio management software.

Quonetra is not account management software.

Quonetra is not responsible for:

- Position sizing
- Bankroll management
- Order execution
- Personal risk management
- Brokerage integration

Those responsibilities belong to separate client applications.

This separation is intentional and fundamental.

---

# 6. User Experience Principles

Users should always understand why Quonetra reached a conclusion.

Recommendations should emphasize understanding rather than automation.

Every recommendation should communicate:

- The conclusion
- Supporting evidence
- Confidence
- Areas of uncertainty
- Alternative interpretations

Users should feel informed rather than directed.

---

# 7. Recommendation Philosophy

Quonetra recommends opportunities.

It does not make decisions.

Recommendations may include:

PASS

WATCH

BUY

HOLD

REDUCE

EXIT

HEDGE

AVOID

Every recommendation should include:

Estimated Fair Value

Current Market Assessment

Expected Edge

Confidence

Reasoning

Supporting Evidence

Applicable Knowledge Objects

Recommendations should be based on independent analysis rather than market consensus.

---

# 8. Evidence & Data Philosophy

Evidence is the foundation of every recommendation.

Forecasts are evidence.

Observations are evidence.

Weather models are evidence.

Market prices are evidence.

Historical outcomes are evidence.

None of these represent truth.

No evidence source is privileged.

The quality of a recommendation depends upon the quality, diversity, and interpretation of evidence.

---

# 9. Canonical Data Standards

Data integrity is critical.

Quonetra stores data using canonical representations.

Examples include:

Temperature → Celsius

Pressure → millibars / hectopascals

Wind → knots

Distance → kilometers

Visibility → kilometers

Time → UTC with associated time zone metadata

Display preferences are independent from storage.

User interfaces may display Fahrenheit, miles, or other preferred units without affecting stored data.

Raw observations should remain immutable.

Derived values should always be reproducible.

---

# 10. AI Design Principles

Artificial Intelligence is an implementation detail, not the product.

Quonetra should support multiple AI providers and reasoning systems.

No model should be permanently privileged.

Every model must earn its place through measurable performance.

Prompt engineering, model selection, reasoning strategies, and feature extraction are all subject to experimentation.

Every AI decision should be explainable.

---

# 11. Knowledge Objects

Knowledge is a first-class entity.

Knowledge Objects represent validated discoveries accumulated through observation, experimentation, and experience.

Examples include:

Heuristics

Patterns

Statistical relationships

Lessons learned

Operational rules

Environmental behaviors

Each Knowledge Object should include:

Description

Evidence

Supporting cases

Confidence

Applicable conditions

Version history

Validation history

Knowledge Objects are living entities.

They may be strengthened, weakened, revised, or retired as new evidence becomes available.

---

# 12. Learning & Continuous Improvement

Quonetra continuously evaluates itself.

It measures:

Prediction quality

Calibration

Recommendation quality

Knowledge quality

Prompt quality

Model quality

Data source contribution

Experiment performance

Every improvement begins as a hypothesis.

Every hypothesis becomes an experiment.

Every experiment produces evidence.

Evidence determines adoption.

Opinion does not.

---

# 13. Trust, Explainability & Transparency

Every recommendation should answer:

What does the evidence suggest?

What is Quonetra's conclusion?

Why?

How confident is the conclusion?

What evidence most influenced it?

Which Knowledge Objects contributed?

What assumptions remain uncertain?

If Quonetra cannot explain a recommendation, that recommendation should not be trusted.

---

# 14. Extensibility Principles

Quonetra should never assume a single domain.

The platform should naturally support additional prediction markets.

New domains should be added through configuration rather than architectural change.

Components should remain modular, independently testable, and replaceable.

New evidence sources, AI models, experiments, and recommendation strategies should integrate without modifying the core philosophy.

---

# 15. Non-Goals

Quonetra does not attempt to eliminate uncertainty.

Quonetra does not guarantee profitability.

Quonetra does not replace human judgment.

Quonetra does not optimize for trading frequency.

Quonetra does not chase market sentiment.

Quonetra does not automate financial decisions.

Correctly recognizing uncertainty is often more valuable than making a prediction.

---

# 16. Roadmap Philosophy

Development should prioritize depth before breadth.

The platform should master a narrow problem before expanding into additional markets.

Every new capability should strengthen the core intelligence platform.

Features that do not improve understanding, knowledge, or recommendation quality should be questioned.

The architecture should evolve through measured improvement rather than large redesigns.

---

# 17. Governance

This Constitution defines enduring principles.

Implementation details belong in supporting documents.

Architectural decisions belong in Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).

Experimental procedures belong in research documentation.

API behavior belongs in API specifications.

Database design belongs in technical documentation.

The Constitution should change rarely.

Supporting documents should evolve frequently.

---

# Closing Principle

Quonetra exists to discover truth through evidence.

Every observation is evidence.

Every prediction is a hypothesis.

Every outcome is a lesson.

Every lesson becomes knowledge.

Every piece of knowledge improves future understanding.

The success of Quonetra will not be measured by the number of predictions it makes.

It will be measured by whether tomorrow it understands the world better than it did today.
