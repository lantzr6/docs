## Background

Mobile App v2 is the second major version of the flagship mobile application, driven by feedback collected from v1 users over the past year. The core focus areas are a significant performance overhaul — targeting faster cold-start times and smoother navigation transitions — and a fully redesigned onboarding flow that reduces drop-off at the sign-up stage. Early user research highlighted friction in the initial setup steps, and v2 aims to address this with a streamlined, contextual onboarding experience.

## Proposed Features

- **Push notifications** — customisable alerts for activity updates and reminders
- **Offline mode** — core read and note-taking features available without a network connection
- **Dark mode** — system-aware theme switching with a manual override option
- **Redesigned onboarding** — step-by-step guided setup with progress indicators and skip options
- **Biometric authentication** — Face ID and fingerprint login support on compatible devices
- **Improved search** — full-text search across all content with filters and recent history
