Project Notes is a personal knowledge base for organizing notes across multiple projects. Whether you're tracking project updates, logging meeting notes, storing research findings, or capturing ideas before they slip away, everything lives in one structured, searchable place — so you spend less time hunting and more time doing.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the Templates">
    Head to the Templates section and copy a template that fits your note type — whether that's a full project, a meeting recap, or a research deep-dive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your project page">
    Duplicate the template under Projects and fill in your details: goals, status, key dates, and any notes you already have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay organized">
    Use the Archive to file completed work when a project wraps up, and drop future thoughts onto the Ideas page so nothing gets lost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use the search bar at the top to quickly find any note, project, or idea across your entire knowledge base.
</Tip>
